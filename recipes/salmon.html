Sheet Pan Salmon and Bacon Wrapped Asparagus

This one pan recipe will feed you and your family efficiently, and will avoid making a ton of unwanted dishes!

Ingredients needed:

  • 4 Salmon Fillets
  • 1 bundle of Asparagus
  • 1 pound of Bacon
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Black Pepper

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit. Line baking sheet with two horizontal pieces of aluminum foil.
  2. Place salmon filets on sheet pan, lightly drizzle with oil, and season generously with Paprika, Garlic Powder, and Black Pepper.
  3. Cut the bottom part of the Asparagus off, and open pack of bacon.
  4. Grab 2-4 Asparagus, then begin wrapping with a single piece of bacon, and once wrapped place on the second piece of foil on the sheet tray.
  5. Once finished, place into oven for 10-14 minutes, watching the Salmon, because it will be finished first.
  6. Once the Salmon is done, pull it off onto a cutting board to cool while we finish the Asparagus.
  7. Watch for browninining on the bacon, and make sure to roatete to brown evenly, rotate with a long tong-like tool.
  8. Once bacon reaches desired doneness, pull out, save bacon grease in a proper receptacal.
  9. Allow to cool, then serve.
